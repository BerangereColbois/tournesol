Tournesol is temporarily offline

Tournesol aims to identify top videos of public utility by eliciting contributors' judgements on content quality. Check-out our open source code, or join our Discord.

Tournesol is currently offline, because we detected serious security vulnerabilities in our code base. We are running a thorough refactoring and audit of our backend codes (written with Django) to restart on more solid grounds. Unfortunately, because we are a small team of mostly volunteers, this effort may take us a couple of months.

If you can, please consider helping us, through coding or through donations to safely get Tournesol back online as soon as possible.
